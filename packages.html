Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for weekend events. However, we sometimes have last-minute availability!
What kind of balloons do you use?
We use 100% biodegradable, high-quality professional latex balloons that are sustainably sourced.
Do you travel outside of the city?
Yes! We travel nationwide for large corporate events. Travel fees apply for locations more than 30 miles from our studio.
Is twisting suitable for outdoor events?
Yes, but extreme heat or direct sunlight can cause balloons to pop. We recommend a shaded area for the best results.
What happens if an artist is sick?
We have a team of backup artists and a strict protocol to ensure your event is never left without entertainment.
